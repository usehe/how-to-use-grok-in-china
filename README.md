## What is Grok / SuperGrok

**Grok** is the AI model built by **xAI** (Elon Musk's company), tightly integrated with **X (formerly Twitter)**. Its strengths are real-time web/X access, awareness of current events, and a relatively open, creative style — which makes it popular with content creators and people who want a news-aware assistant. For heavy software engineering, dedicated coding tools are generally stronger, so treat Grok as a complement there rather than a primary.

**SuperGrok** is the paid subscription that lifts the free tier's rate limits and unlocks more capability. As of mid-2026 the latest publicly released model is **Grok 4.3**, with a larger next-generation model reported to be on the way. Which model each tier unlocks is rolled out in stages — check the official site.

## The real blocker in China: payment

Registering Grok is easy. The wall most users in mainland China hit is **payment**:

> Grok's subscription billing does **not** accept Alipay, WeChat Pay, or domestic Chinese bank cards. It only accepts **overseas credit cards** (Visa / Mastercard, etc.). Users without such a card are stuck at the payment page; even those with one are often declined by risk control.

Everything below is about getting past that cleanly — without falling for scams.
